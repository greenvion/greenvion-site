Cooling Buildings Naturally

GreenVion is a bio-integrated façade retrofit solution designed to reduce heat gain, improve thermal comfort, and lower cooling energy demand in buildings across warm climates.

Explore the Solution

About GreenVion

GreenVion develops sustainable façade retrofit systems for existing buildings. The solution combines vegetation, shading, and passive cooling strategies to reduce dependency on air-conditioning while improving environmental performance and occupant comfort.

The Problem

High solar heat gain through building envelopes
Increasing electricity consumption from cooling systems
Urban heat island effect in dense cities
Growing operational cost and carbon emissions

Our Solution

Modular façade retrofit system
Integrated greenery and passive shading
Adaptable for existing windows and façades
Scalable for residential, institutional and commercial buildings

Pilot Project

Initial proof-of-concept installation across 10 rooms to monitor measurable performance including indoor temperature reduction, cooling energy savings, carbon reduction, and user comfort improvements.
